# Generalized crown positioning (novelty-theory-lean)

**Purpose:** State what the **`EPIC_012`** (“generalized crown frontier”) layer **adds** on top of **`FinalCrownPackage`** (**`EPIC_011`**), and what remains **out of scope**. Normative execution: **`EPIC_012_GENERALIZED_CROWN_FRONTIER`** in the **[companion repo](https://github.com/novaspivack/novelty-theory/tree/main/specs/INCOMPLETE/IN-PROCESS/EPIC_012_GENERALIZED_CROWN_FRONTIER/)**.

## What is proved (additive scope)

- **Aligned carrier semantics (`SPEC_048_GC1`):** Under **`AlignsCarrier G C`**, **`carrierHolds`** and **`sentenceCarrierEmbed`** interact with **`HoldsAt`** / **`ProvesAt`** as in **[`PhaseGeneralizationFacts`](../NoveltyTheory/Foundation/PhaseGeneralizationFacts.lean)**—including **`ProvesAtCarrier`** strict gaps packaging enum crowns at **`Bool × ℕ`**.
- **Structural sentence v2 (`SPEC_049_GC2`):** **`IsStructuralSentenceV2`** unions generator-structural and retro v2 fragments; **[`StructuralSentenceHierarchyV2`](../NoveltyTheory/Foundation/StructuralSentenceHierarchyV2.lean)** gives anti-collapse lemmas and **`structuralV2_crown_family`**.
- **Modal / temporal scaffold (`SPEC_050_GC3`):** Stage-relative modal definitions over **`ProvesAt`** with soundness and modalized links to crown-scale sentences (**[`ModalTemporalFacts`](../NoveltyTheory/Foundation/ModalTemporalFacts.lean)**).
- **Abstract bounded organization (`SPEC_051_GC4`):** **`AdmissibleBoundedOrganization`** feeds **[`OrganizationAbstractObstruction`](../NoveltyTheory/Ridge/OrganizationAbstractObstruction.lean)**—height-disciplined **¬ `TotalizesStandardFuture`** packaging used in the generalized summit.
- **Admissible-class transfer (`SPEC_052_GC5`):** Two **trace-distinguishable** **`GenerativeSystem ℕ (Bool × ℕ)`** models, each carrier-aligned, **preserve** enum-crown **`ProvesAtCarrier`** gaps (**[`CrownTransfer`](../NoveltyTheory/Ridge/CrownTransfer.lean)**)—so the phenomenon is **not** tied to a **single** trace type.
- **Generalized final package (`SPEC_053_GC6`):** **[`GeneralizedFinalCrownPackage`](../NoveltyTheory/Summits/GeneralizedFinalCrownPackage.lean)** composes **v2** ascent, abstract org obstruction, **`FutureDefeat`**, generator-truth linkage (**via `FinalCrownPackage`**), class transfer, and non-singleton model evidence.

## What is not claimed (boundaries)

- **Same global obstructions as `FinalCrownPackage`:** no literal universal diagonal over **all** raw **`AdmissibleInterface`** values; no infinite paradigm chain for **every** **`RegimeFamilySingletonWithin`** family. See **[`CROWN_POSITIONING.md`](CROWN_POSITIONING.md)** and the inventory “blocked form” anchors.
- **Class transfer is existential and constructed:** the **`true` / `alt`** **`Bool × ℕ`** pair proves **compatibility** of crown gaps with **observational disagreement**—**not** a theorem that **every** admissible class preserves **all** crown facts.
- **Proof-theoretic stance unchanged:** claims remain relative to the **defined** **`ProvesAt`** calculus (Model **C** depth), not arbitrary external logics.
