# EPIC_009 — Strict iterated ascent (crown explanatory incompleteness)

**Goal:** Move from **stratified proof-height facts** and bounded-stage obstructions to **crown-level** statements: an explicit family \((\Phi_n)\) of sentences that are **genuinely about** a fixed generator \(G\), with **strict derivability ascent** across a matched family of regimes \((R_n)\)—without the result collapsing to a disguised **mention-bound** restatement.

**Motivation (informal):** [`docs/007_LIBRARY_POSITIONING_ASSESSMENT.md`](../../../../docs/007_LIBRARY_POSITIONING_ASSESSMENT.md) (template in §6); full declaration index [`docs/THEOREM_INVENTORY.md`](../../../../docs/THEOREM_INVENTORY.md).

**Depends on:** **EPIC_003**–**EPIC_008** (sentence universe, `ProvesAt`, stratified witnesses, finality summit, retro ridge, abstract simulation vs explanation); **`SPEC_033_K7A`** closure as baseline.

**Blocks:** None declared—this epic **extends** the closed tranche.
